🌟 Features

  • Multiplayer word chain gameplay - compete with a friend in real-time.
  • Online multiplayer using MQTT protocol for seamless networking.
  • Dynamic turn-based system with 30-second timer per turn.
  • Strike system - 3 strikes and you're out!
  • Real-time scoring and player elimination.
  • Beautiful forest-themed interface with intuitive design.
  • Lightweight installer with quick, easy setup.

🎮 How to Play

  • Join a Room: Enter your player name and either create a new room or join an existing one using a 6-letter room ID.
  • Starting Word: Once both players have joined, the game automatically selects the starting player and required letter.
  • Word Chain Rule: Your word must start with the letter shown on screen (the last letter of the previous word).
  • Valid Words Only: All words must be valid English words. Invalid words result in a strike.
  • No Repeats: You cannot use a word that has already been played in the current round.
  • Time Pressure: You have 30 seconds per turn. Submit your word before time runs out!
  • Strikes: Receive 3 strikes and you're eliminated from the game. Strikes are given for: submitting when it's not your turn, using invalid words, repeating words, or not starting with the required letter.
  • Winning: The last player standing wins the game!

📝 Example Gameplay

  • Player 1: Submits "TREE" (starts with T)
  • Player 2: Must submit a word starting with E - enters "ELEPHANT"
  • Player 1: Must submit a word starting with T - enters "TABLE"
  • Player 2: Must submit a word starting with E - and so on...
  • If a player can't think of a valid word or breaks the rules, they receive a strike!
