📖 About The Game

Word Chain Multiplayer is an online 1v1 game where players take turns entering valid English words.

Every new word must begin with the final letter of the previous word. The match continues until one player is eliminated by strikes or leaves the room.

✨ Features

  • 🌐 Online Multiplayer Rooms

  • 📚 Large English Dictionary Validation
  • ⏱ 30 Second Turn Timer
  • ❌ Strike System
  • 🏆 Winner Detection
  • 🔄 Automatic Updates
  • 📦 One Click Installation

🎮 How To Play

  1. Download and run the launcher.
  2. Create a room or join a friend's room.
  3. Wait until two players are connected.
  4. The game starts automatically.
  5. Enter a valid English word using the required letter.
  6. The next player must continue from your last letter.
  7. Invalid words give strikes.
  8. Exceed the strike limit and you are eliminated.
  9. The last remaining player wins the match.

💻 Requirements
